Why Sri Lanka

Small Island. Infinite Wonder.

Sri Lanka is one of the most diverse destinations on the planet, packed into an island you can drive across in a day. From the UNESCO-listed ancient cities of the Cultural Triangle to the biodiverse jungles of Sinharaja, from the emerald tea estates of the hill country to the palm-fringed beaches of the south coast — every corner of this island holds a story.

At HighFlyer Travel, we are Sri Lankan to our core. Nobody knows this island better than we do — and nobody will show it to you with more pride.

Must-See Experiences

Sri Lanka's Finest

These are the experiences that make first-time visitors become lifelong Sri Lanka fans.

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

A 5th-century palace rising 200m above the jungle. One of Asia's most dramatic ancient sites and a UNESCO World Heritage treasure.

Yala National Park

Home to the world's highest density of leopards, plus elephants, sloth bears, and hundreds of bird species. A safari unlike any other.

Ella & The Nine Arch Bridge

The hill town of Ella is a traveller's paradise — misty mountains, the iconic Nine Arch Bridge, and the world's most scenic train ride.

Kandy & The Temple of the Tooth

Sri Lanka's cultural capital is home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic, set against the backdrop of a tranquil lake and lush hills.

Southern Coast Beaches

Mirissa, Unawatuna, and Tangalle offer world-class beaches with crystal-clear waters, excellent surf, and spectacular whale watching from November to April.

Tea Country

Nuwara Eliya's rolling emerald estates produce some of the world's finest Ceylon tea. Tour a plantation, visit a factory, and sip a perfect cup at the source.
