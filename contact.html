FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you book a flight ticket? +

Most flight bookings are confirmed within a few hours of your enquiry. For urgent same-day bookings, call us directly on +94 77 919 1001.

Do you arrange visas? +

We provide guidance on visa requirements and documentation for all popular destinations. While we don't process visas directly, we'll make sure you know exactly what you need and when.

Can you handle group bookings? +

Absolutely. We regularly manage group travel for families, corporate retreats, and tour groups. The larger the group, the better the rates we can negotiate on your behalf.

What's included in your tour packages? +

Each package varies, but typically includes flights, accommodation, airport transfers, guided excursions, and 24/7 support. We'll give you a full breakdown when we send your quote.
