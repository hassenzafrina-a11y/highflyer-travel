Our Story

Born from a Shared Love of Travel

HighFlyer Travel didn't start in a boardroom. It started with three friends, a shared frustration, and a stubborn belief that travel should be effortless — not exhausting.

Fahad, Sasanka, and Zafrina had each experienced the same thing: the joy of discovering a new destination, followed by the unnecessary stress of getting there. Complicated bookings, confusing itineraries, agencies that treated clients like numbers — it didn't have to be this way.

So in 2024, they built the agency they always wished existed. One where every traveller feels like a VIP. One where flights, tours, and visa headaches are handled so you can focus on what matters: the experience.

Today, HighFlyer Travel is Sri Lanka's go-to partner for outbound journeys and a proud showcase of our island's magic to the world's inbound visitors. We've helped thousands of travellers fly higher — and we're just getting started.

Our Values

Three principles guide everything we do at HighFlyer Travel.

Travel planning shouldn't feel like a second job. We strip away the complexity and deliver clarity — from the first enquiry to the final boarding call.

Good enough isn't good enough. We hold ourselves to a standard of quality and care that turns first-time clients into lifelong travel companions.
